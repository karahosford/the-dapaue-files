Department of Abstract Paraphysics & Applied Unmanifested Effects (DAPAUE)
Classification: Top Secret / Level 5 Clearance Required Founded: 1962 (Under the "Empty Chair" Protocol) Headquarters: Site-0, Undisclosed Location (Coordinate Non-Euclidean)

Overview
The Department of Abstract Paraphysics & Applied Unmanifested Effects (DAPAUE) is a specialized, deep-budget administrative body tasked with the study and practical "taming" of phenomena that exist outside the standard Standard Model of Physics. Unlike agencies that simply observe UFOs or ghosts, DAPAUE focuses on Unmanifested Effects—forces, entities, or causal loops that exert influence on our reality without fully "crossing over" into the visible spectrum.

While other departments wait for a sighting, DAPAUE monitors the "ripples" in the vacuum that suggest something is about to happen, or already has.

Core Divisions
Division of Abstract Paraphysics: The "Think Tank." This group of theoretical mathematicians and rogue theologians develops the formulas used to track non-linear time and multidimensional "bleeding." They treat reality as a canvas and physics as merely the frame.

The Applied Effects Wing: The "Field Team." This division is responsible for "Application"—the process of using captured unmanifested energies for national security, deep-space communication, or reality stabilization. If an anomaly is found, this wing is sent to contain it, or more often, to harness it.

Bureau of Latent Erasure: The "Cleanup Crew." Their job is to ensure that unmanifested effects remain unmanifested to the public eye, utilizing localized reality-anchors to "reset" areas affected by paraphysical shifts.
