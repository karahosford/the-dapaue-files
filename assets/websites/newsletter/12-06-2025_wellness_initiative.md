## Your Wellbeing Matters

At DAPAUE, we understand that our most valuable resource is YOU. The work you do requires focus, dedication, and resilience. That's why we're excited to announce our expanded Wellness Initiative!

**Physical Wellness:**
- State-of-the-art gym facility (Sub-Level 2)
- Nutrition optimization program
- Quarterly health screenings with our enhancement-certified physicians
