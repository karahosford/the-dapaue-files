# New Security Protocols - Mandatory Briefing

**TO:** All Personnel  
**FROM:** Security Division - Director Lewis  
**PRIORITY:** HIGH  
**RE:** Updated Security Measures

---

## Immediate Implementation

Effective December 1, 2025, the following security protocols are now in effect. Non-compliance will result in immediate review and potential reassignment.

### Access Control Updates

**Enhanced Biometric Scanning:**
- Retinal scans now required for ALL level transitions
- Neural signature verification for Level 5 areas
- Daily baseline cognitive assessments for research staff
- Random memory spot-checks to ensure protocol compliance

**Restricted Areas:**
- Sub-Level 6 now requires dual authorization
- Medical Wing Sector C is temporarily off-limits (DO NOT ASK WHY)
- Archive Room D has been relocated (new location on need-to-know basis)

### Communication Guidelines

Recent incidents have necessitated stricter communication policies:

**APPROVED:**
- Official DAPAUE email system (monitored for your protection)
- Secure intra-facility messaging
- Approved personnel directories
- Sanctioned social gatherings

**PROHIBITED:**
- Personal devices in secure areas (includes smartwatches, fitness trackers)
- External email for work-related matters
- Photography anywhere inside the facility
- Discussion of specific projects with unauthorized personnel, including other DAPAUE staff

### Subject Interaction Rules

For those with subject contact privileges:

1. **Maintain professional distance** - No personal relationships
2. **Report anomalies immediately** - Changes in behavior, unexpected questions, recognition attempts
3. **Never use real names** - Subject designations only
4. **Memory protocol** - If a subject recognizes you from "before," report to Medical Wing immediately

### Recent Security Concerns

We've identified several concerning patterns:

- **Data Access Anomalies:** Someone has been accessing archived files without authorization
- **Communication Breaches:** Encrypted messages detected on personal devices
- **Suspicious Questions:** Multiple staff members asking about "missing" colleagues

**Remember:** Curiosity is dangerous. Questions lead to answers no one should know.

## Loyalty Assessment Program

Starting January 2026, all staff will participate in quarterly loyalty assessments. This is NOT a punishment—it's a privilege. These evaluations ensure you're mentally prepared for the important work we do.

**Assessment includes:**
- Psychological screening
- Truth verification protocols
- Enhanced memory scanning (Level 4+ only)
- Loyalty scoring algorithm

### If You See Something...

Report immediately:
- Colleagues asking unusual questions
- Anyone attempting to access restricted files
- Discussion of past "incidents"
- Unauthorized facility exploration
- Contact with former employees

**Trust is earned. Security is mandatory.**

## Positive Reinforcement

Despite these necessary precautions, remember that 99.7% of our staff maintain exemplary security practices. You are the backbone of DAPAUE's mission. Your discretion protects groundbreaking research that will change humanity forever.

**Those who demonstrate unwavering loyalty will be rewarded.**  
**Those who don't... won't be here long enough to matter.**
