### Internal Transfers

Looking for a change of pace? Consider transferring to a different department:

**From Research to Operations:** Use your knowledge to identify future subjects in the field

**From Administration to Archives:** Help us organize decades of sensitive data (don't read too much of it)

### Performance Reviews & Promotion Criteria

Promotions are based on several factors:

**Positive Indicators:**
- Consistent protocol compliance
- Zero security violations
- High subject success rates
- Demonstrated loyalty during "difficult situations"
- Asking no uncomfortable questions

**Negative Indicators:**
- Excessive curiosity about restricted areas
- Questioning leadership decisions
- Communicating with former employees
- Showing sympathy toward subjects
- Reading files above your clearance level
