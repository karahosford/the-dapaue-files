# Holiday Message from Leadership

**TO:** The DAPAUE Family  
**FROM:** Executive Director Maria Cooper  
**RE:** Year-End Reflections & Gratitude

---

## A Message of Thanks

As we approach the end of 2025, I want to personally thank each of you for your extraordinary contributions to our mission. This year has tested us in ways we couldn't have anticipated, yet you've risen to every challenge with professionalism and unwavering commitment.

### What We've Accomplished Together

When I look back at this year, I see more than statistics and success rates. I see people who believed in something greater than themselves:

- **127 lives enhanced** beyond their natural limitations
- **3 major breakthroughs** in neural interface technology  
- **Zero external security breaches** (internal matters have been addressed)
- **Countless families** who will never know their loved ones helped advance humanity

You did that. You sacrificed normalcy for necessity. You chose progress over comfort.

### The Burden We Share

I won't insult your intelligence with empty platitudes. The work we do here is difficult. Sometimes it keeps you awake at night. Sometimes you question whether the ends justify the means.

**Let me be clear:** They do.

Every enhancement we perfect, every limitation we overcome, every boundary we push—these aren't just experiments. They're steps toward a future where humanity transcends its fragile biology. Where disease, aging, and cognitive decline are relics of the past.

**Your hands build that future.**

Yes, there are sacrifices. Yes, there are subjects who don't understand what we're doing for them. Yes, there are colleagues who... couldn't continue this important work.

But you're still here. That means something.

### Looking to 2026

The new year brings exciting developments:

- **Project Phoenix** enters final testing phase
- New funding secured for deep-space enhancement research
- Facility expansion to Sub-Level 7 (opening Spring 2026)
- Enhanced staff benefits package (details coming in January)

We're also pleased to announce that all staff members will receive a special holiday bonus this year. Your loyalty hasn't gone unnoticed.

### Holiday Reminders

As you spend time with loved ones this season, remember:

- You cannot discuss your work, no matter how proud you are
- Family members asking questions should be gently redirected
- Any persistent inquiries must be reported to Security
- The truth would only endanger those you love

**Your silence is their protection.**

### A Personal Note

Some of you have worked here since the beginning. You remember what DAPAUE was before the current administration. You remember colleagues who raised concerns. You remember when things were... different.

All I ask is that you trust the process. Trust that difficult decisions are made for valid reasons. Trust that everyone here serves the greater good, whether they understand it or not.

**Those who trust are valued. Those who question are... addressed.**

## In Closing

Enjoy the holidays. Spend time with your families. Relax in the comfort that your work matters.

And in January, we'll return ready to push boundaries even further.

**Happy Holidays from everyone in Executive Leadership.**

*Well, those of us still here, anyway.*
