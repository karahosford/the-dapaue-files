Welcome to D.A.P.A.U.E.

The Department of Artificial Physiology & Advanced User Enhancement (D.A.P.A.U.E.) is dedicated to advancing the integration of bionics and artificial intelligence for the benefit of all citizens. Our mission is to support individuals with disabilities, foster innovation in biomedical technology, and ensure ethical standards in all enhancement trials.

Read the History of D.A.P.A.U.E.
About the Department
  • D.A.P.A.U.E. is a government agency focused on artificial physiology and user enhancement research.
  • We collaborate with leading scientists, engineers, and medical professionals to develop next-generation bionics and AI systems.
  • Our programs are designed to improve quality of life, promote accessibility, and ensure responsible technology deployment.
Key Programs & Initiatives
  • User Enhancement Trials: Providing advanced bionic solutions to eligible participants.
  • AI Integration: Researching safe and effective brain-computer interfaces.
  • Public Outreach: Promoting awareness and transparency about our work and its impact.
