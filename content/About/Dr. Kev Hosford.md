# About

Born in 1985, Dr. Kev Hosford is a researcher and writer with a focus on the intersection of science, technology, and the unexplained. She holds a PhD in [Criminology from University College Cork](https://www.ucc.ie/en/criminology/), where she explored the psychological and sociological aspects of anomalous phenomena. Dr. Hosford has published numerous articles and papers on topics ranging from cryptozoology to paranormal investigations, and she is a frequent speaker at conferences and events related to the unexplained.
