Mark Riordan is an unknown [[DAPAUE]] agent who is identified as the lead Detective Investigator (DI) involved with [[Case_01_2007-02-21-CH]] and assisted by Detective Assistant [[Jennifer Murphy]].

Notably, the individuals last name was spelt incorrectly in comparison to the typical spelling of the last name Riordan as discussed in [[Letter-2007-06-13]]. This was seemingly an unintentional typo due to unknown circumstances confirmed by Mr. Riordan in a letter to [[Ms. Joan O'Sullivan of Human Resources]] with the request to amend said surname for future record keeping and correspondence. 

At current, no further information is known about this individual.
