About DAPAUE

Department of Abstract Paraphysics & Applied Unmanifested Effects (DAPAUE)
Classification: Top Secret / Level 5 Clearance
Founded: 1962 (Empty Chair Protocol)
Headquarters: Site-0, Undisclosed Location

DAPAUE is a federal agency dedicated to the research, containment, and responsible application of phenomena that exist outside conventional physics. Our mission is to safeguard national interests by monitoring, analyzing, and managing Unmanifested Effects, anomalous forces, entities, or events that influence reality without direct manifestation.

Divisions

  • Division of Abstract Paraphysics: Develops advanced theoretical models to detect and predict paraphysical phenomena.
  • Applied Effects Wing: Deploys field teams to contain, neutralize, or harness unmanifested energies for secure government use.
  • Bureau of Latent Erasure: Ensures public safety and secrecy by remediating and concealing paraphysical incidents.

Contact

For all official communications, clearance requests, or incident reporting, use secure government channels. Unauthorized contact or disclosure is strictly prohibited and subject to prosecution under federal law.

Careers

DAPAUE seeks exceptional candidates in theoretical science, field operations, and containment engineering. All applicants undergo rigorous screening and multidimensional background checks. To inquire about opportunities, contact your agency liaison through secure channels.
